    Use one of the following websites and the inspect tool to identify 2-3 colors used repeatedly as part of the site’s color scheme. Remember that different shades of gray and black can be included as a color

    Result

    The selected website is Sebkay. The color palette seems to be a mix of white, grey and blue color tones:

    • Main links font color: rgba(30, 41, 59, 1)
    • Sub links font color: rgba(100, 116, 139, 1)

    UI/UX 3

    Statement

    Create user stories by referencing one of these websites

    Result

    The selected website is Youtube. Below are the proposed user stories. The first one is an example of the followed structure:

    • As a [user type], I want to [action] to [value] -> [funtionality]
    • As a user, I want to play a video to watch content of my interest -> Play
    • As a user, I want to leave a comment on a video to share opinions with the community -> Comment
    • As a user, I want to search for videos to find relevant content -> Search
    • As a user, I want to subscribe to channels to receive notifications about new videos -> Subscribe
    • As a content creator, I want to upload videos to share them with my audience -> Upload

    UI/UX 4

    Statement

    Identify the prime objective of Facebook, Twitter, Google, and Youtube

    Result

    • Facebook: Connect people and allow them to share content, stay updated with friends and communities, and engage with various types of media

    • Twitter: Facilitate real-time communication, news sharing, and discussions through short, concise messages (tweets)

    • Google: Provide users relevant search results and organize the world’s information for easy access and use

    • Youtube: Allow users to upload, share, and discover videos, fostering engagement through comments, likes, and subscriptions
